Building my ergodox

In this post I give a short description of how I built my ergodox: Sourcing parts I bought the PCB, key switches and teensy from mechanicalkeyboards.com The rest of the electronic components I bought from digikey, by following the links from the ergodox website. I bought a TRRS connector from mycablemart. (see the Problems (See section ) section below) I bought the key caps from signature plastics. With this set you get 2 deep dish keys (for F and J) which the massdrop kits do not include.
